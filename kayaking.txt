Popular Kayaking Spots and Services in the Pacific West
Washington State
San Juan Islands – Known for calm waters and orca sightings. Companies like Discovery Sea Kayaks and San Juan Outfitters offer guided tours.

Lake Union (Seattle) – A fun urban spot with views of houseboats and the skyline. Rentals available from Agua Verde Paddle Club and Northwest Outdoor Center.

Deception Pass State Park – A beautiful but advanced spot with strong currents.

Moran State Park (Orcas Island) – Peaceful lake paddling in the forest.

Oregon
Columbia River Gorge – Offers both relaxing and challenging routes depending on the section.

Hood River – Great mix of kayaking and windsurfing culture.

Alder Creek Kayak & Canoe (Portland) – Offers gear rentals, lessons, and tours for all skill levels.

Northern California
Russian River (Sonoma) – Calm, beginner-friendly water flowing through redwoods and wine country.

Tomales Bay (Point Reyes) – Peaceful paddling with chances to spot wildlife.

Klamath River – A newly restored river ideal for scenic and nature-filled kayaking.
